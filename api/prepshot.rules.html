power_balance_rule(h, m, y, z)¶

Power balance. Note: The total electricity demand for each time period and in each zone should be met by the following. 1. The sum of imported power energy from other zones. 2. The generation from zone z minus the sum of exported power energy from zone z to other zones. 3. The charging power energy of storage technologies in zone z.
