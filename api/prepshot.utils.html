PREP-SHOT.utils¶

This module contains utility functions for the model.

Calculate the error of the water head.

Parameters¶

old_waterheadpandas.DataFrame

The water head before the solution.

new_waterheadpandas.DataFrame

The water head after the solution.

Returns¶

float

The error of the water head.

Parameters¶

discount_ratefloat

Discount rate.

modeled_yearint

Modeled year.

year_minint

Minimum year.

next_modeled_yearint

Next modeled year.

Returns¶

float

The cost factor.

Raises¶

if next_modeled_year is less than modeled_year.

Whether the model should consider hydrological constraints. Defaults to True., by default True

A Dataset containing DataArrays for each attribute of the model.

prepshot.utils.inv_cost_factor(dep_period, interest_rate, year_built, discount_rate, year_min, year_max)¶

Calculate the investment cost factor.

Parameters¶

dep_periodint

Depreciation period.

interest_ratefloat

Interest rate.

year_builtint

Year of construction.

discount_ratefloat

Discount rate.

year_minint

Minimum year.

year_maxint

Maximum year.

Returns¶

float

The investment cost factor.

Raises¶

ValueError

If year_max is less than or equal to year_min, or year_max is less than year_built, or year_built is less than year_min.
